# Shape Up! Studies

Exploring how 2D and 3D images of the body can address and improve our understanding of metabolic disease, obesity, and their relationships to cancer.

The Shape Up! Studies use advanced body-imaging technology to develop methods for using body shape to assess overall health — across three studies covering different life stages.

## Procedures

All three studies include questionnaires, body measurements, DXA, BIA, and 3D optical scans.

| Cohort | Adds |
|---|---|
| Keiki | Urine / saliva |
| Kids | Urine / saliva, strength tests, blood draw |
| Adults | Blood draw, MRI scan |
