## Review and approval process

1. The policy guides tracking of all publications and grant applications using Shape Up! data.
2. Investigators submit a form outlining the request — abstract, manuscript, or grant.
3. Principal Investigators review and approve.
4. The database tracks all ongoing projects with monthly status updates.
5. New variables derived from the data are returned to the study database.

</div>
</section>

<div class="container" markdown="1" style="max-width: 820px;">

## Manuscript requirements

- One Shape Up! Principal Investigator must be listed as last author
- All funding sources must be acknowledged
- Publication costs may be covered case-by-case
- A data-availability statement must be provided to protect participant confidentiality

## Grant requirements

- Biological-sample requests require peer-reviewed funding
- Outside expert consultants may be engaged as needed
- Dataset preparation and sample retrieval costs are charged to the requesting project
