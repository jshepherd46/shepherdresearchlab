## Eligibility

### Keiki — actively enrolling

- Birth through 5 years old
- No removable metal in or on body
- No prior major body-shape-altering surgeries

### Kids and Adults — currently closed

Sign up for our [mailing list]({{ site.baseurl }}/shapeup/newsletter/) for future enrollment updates.

## Incentives

Adult participants — or the parental/guardian unit of minor participants — receive a gift card on visit completion, plus copies of their data. Some may qualify for follow-up visits with additional compensation.

## Procedure

Single visits run 2–5 hours depending on cohort. All participants complete questionnaires, body measurements, DXA scans, BIA, and 3D optical scans. See [scans &amp; tests]({{ site.baseurl }}/shapeup/scans-tests/) for the per-cohort breakdown.
