## What is a muscle dynamometer?

The muscle dynamometer enables **22 isolated joint movement patterns in four resistance modes** — isokinetic, isometric, isotonic, and passive. This lets researchers measure true muscle activation through joint isolation, with both concentric and eccentric resistance options.

## What to expect

You'll be positioned and secured in the dynamometer based on which joint is being assessed. The procedure typically takes **15–20 minutes** depending on the protocol.
