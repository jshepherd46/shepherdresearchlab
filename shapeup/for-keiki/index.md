## What happens during the visit

### Questionnaire (~15 minutes)

Parents complete a questionnaire covering diet and development, including:

- **ASA24** — the Automated Self-Administered 24-hour Dietary Assessment Tool
- **Ages and Stages** — developmental questionnaire

### Body composition

- **DXA scan** — safe for all ages; radiation exposure less than a chest X-ray. Babies may be swaddled for comfort. [More about DXA →]({{ site.baseurl }}/shapeup/dxa-scan/)
- **Bod Pod / Pea Pod** — specialized infant equipment lets us measure children while they lie down. [More about Bod Pod →]({{ site.baseurl }}/shapeup/bodpod/)
- **3D optical scan** — uses a clear crib for full-body scanning without standing. [More about 3D optical →]({{ site.baseurl }}/shapeup/3d-optical-scans/)

### Biological samples

Urine and saliva are collected using the **deuterium dilution method**. Children drink water enriched with deuterium — a safe, measurable isotope — which lets us calculate body composition.
