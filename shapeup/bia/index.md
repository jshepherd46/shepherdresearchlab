## What is BIA?

BIA measures body composition using a low multi-frequency electrical current passed through the body. The current's flow is affected by water content — yielding calculations for fat, lean muscle mass, and water distribution. Shape Up! uses **InBody** technology.

## What to expect

You can stand or sit for approximately 10 minutes. Electrodes clip around your ankles and your thumbs / middle fingers. The actual measurement takes about one minute, followed by a detailed report.
