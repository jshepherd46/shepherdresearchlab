## What is Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis (BIA)?

BIA is a non-invasive body analysis test that uses safe, low-level electrical currents transmitted through foot and hand electrodes. The results identify lean mass and fat mass across each body segment — useful inputs for personalized diet and exercise recommendations.

</div>
</section>

<div class="container" markdown="1" style="max-width: 820px;">

## What can I expect?

You'll either stand or sit for approximately 10 minutes while electrodes attach to ankles and thumbs/middle fingers. The actual measurement takes about one minute. Afterward you receive a detailed report of your body-composition analysis.
