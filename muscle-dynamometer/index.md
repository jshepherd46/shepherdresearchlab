## What is a muscle dynamometer?

The muscle dynamometer enables 22 isolated joint movement patterns in four resistance modes — **isokinetic, isometric, isotonic, and passive**. This lets researchers evaluate actual muscle activation through joint isolation testing. Both concentric and eccentric resistance options are available.

## What can I expect?

You will be positioned and secured in the dynamometer based on which joint is being evaluated. Depending on the protocol, each test takes about 15–20 minutes.
