## What is metabolic assessment?

Metabolic assessment measures gas exchange to determine oxygen consumption and energy production.

**VO2max** represents the maximum oxygen utilization during intense exercise and serves as a fitness indicator. It's measured via treadmill exercise and indirect calorimetry.

**Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR)** quantifies the calories needed for basic body functions like respiration and heartbeat. It informs dietary plans for weight-management goals.

## Preparation

**VO2max**

- Wear athletic clothing
- Light snack approximately one hour prior
- Familiarize yourself with treadmill use beforehand

**RMR**

- Schedule a morning appointment for accuracy
- Fast for 6 hours (water permitted)
- Avoid alcohol or nicotine for 4 hours prior
- No vigorous resistance exercise for 14 hours prior

## What to expect

**VO2max** — a graded maximal treadmill exercise test measuring expired gases through a mask, with blood pressure and heart rate monitoring.

**RMR** — approximately 30 minutes lying at rest with a hood covering your head and shoulders, measuring exhaled gases through connected tubing.
