John Shepherd, chief scientific officer at the University of Hawaiʻi Cancer Center, has been named the **B.H. and Alice C. Beams Endowed Professor** at UH Mānoa's John A. Burns School of Medicine (JABSOM).

Shepherd will receive "$60,000 over three years to advance breast cancer awareness, prevention and treatment in Hawaiʻi and the US Affiliated Pacific Island Jurisdictions (USAPI)."
