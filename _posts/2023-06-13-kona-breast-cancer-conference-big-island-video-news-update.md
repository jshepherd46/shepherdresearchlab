A breast-cancer survivor and advocate shared perspectives on healthcare access in Kona during Big Island Video News coverage. The advocate noted that residents often must travel to Oʻahu for screening and follow-up care, and emphasized that increased local availability would enable earlier diagnoses across the island community.

The conference was covered by *Big Island Video News* on June 13, 2023.
