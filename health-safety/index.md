# Our commitment to safety and prevention

The Body Composition Lab is dedicated to preventing the transmission of respiratory diseases — including COVID-19, RSV, and influenza. Visitors can expect sanitized spaces with appropriate distancing and clear safety protocols.

## What we do

- **Vaccinated personnel** — staff immunization requirements
- **Hand-sanitizing stations** — available throughout the facility
- **Masking** — masks worn at all times in the lab
- **Limited capacity** — restricted numbers in exam areas

## What we ask of visitors

- Wear a mask indoors
- Maintain social distance
- Practice regular hand hygiene
- **Stay home if you're symptomatic** — contact us to reschedule
