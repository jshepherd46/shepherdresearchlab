## International Breast Density & Cancer Risk Assessment Workshop (IBDW)

![IBDW 2019]({{ site.baseurl }}/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/IBDW-2019-1024x551.jpg)

The IBDW occurs biennially and offers three days of educational programming. It convenes researchers, academics, and allied health professionals from around the world to share what they know about breast density and what it means for breast-cancer risk.

[Visit breastdensityworkshop.org ↗](https://breastdensityworkshop.org){: .btn .btn-outline-primary}

## Advanced Stage Breast Cancer Workshop (ABC Workshop)

A workshop addressing advanced breast-cancer stages — including definitions, incidence, and novel AI / machine-learning applications for studying risk and survival outcomes.

[Visit ABC Workshop site ↗](https://breastdensityworkshop.org/abc){: .btn .btn-outline-primary}
